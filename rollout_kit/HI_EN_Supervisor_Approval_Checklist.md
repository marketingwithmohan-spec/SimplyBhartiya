# Supervisor Approval Checklist (Hindi + English)

## 1) Start of Day
- Review yesterday closing report.
- Kal ka closing report review karein.
- Check pending corrections.
- Pending correction requests check karein.

## 2) Before Approval
- Verify original entry and physical proof.
- Original entry aur physical proof verify karein.
- Verify quantity, unit, and reason.
- Quantity, unit aur reason verify karein.

## 3) Escalation Cases
- High-value item correction.
- Backdated or repeated corrections.
- High quantity changes.

## 4) Shift End
- Review daily inward/outward totals.
- Daily inward/outward totals check karein.
- Spot-check critical physical stock.
- Critical stock ka spot check karein.

## 5) Weekly Review
- Correction count by user.
- Stock mismatch incidents.
- Stock-out incidents.
