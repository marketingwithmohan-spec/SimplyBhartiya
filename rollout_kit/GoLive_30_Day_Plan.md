# 30-Day Go-Live Plan (Simple, Ground-Friendly)

## Goal
Go live at one location with stable daily use and accurate stock records.

## Week 1: Preparation (Day 1-7)

### Day 1
- Finalize pilot location.
- Confirm 3 roles: Operator, Supervisor, Manager.

### Day 2
- Freeze item master format: item name, unit, min stock.
- Remove duplicate item names.

### Day 3
- Upload opening stock.
- Validate top 20 critical items physically.

### Day 4
- Train operators (session 1: inward/outward).
- Train supervisors (approval flow).

### Day 5
- Dry run with sample transactions.
- Fix label/language confusion in screens.

### Day 6
- Print SOP and checklists near workstation.
- Confirm support contact list.

### Day 7
- Readiness review.
- Sign go-live readiness by supervisor.

## Week 2: Controlled Go-Live (Day 8-14)

### Day 8-10
- Start live data entry.
- Run paper register and system in parallel.
- Compare closing stock daily.

### Day 11-12
- Remove common errors (wrong unit, delayed entry, missing reason).
- 15-minute refresher training per shift.

### Day 13-14
- Reduce dependency on paper process.
- Keep paper only as backup.

## Week 3: Stabilization (Day 15-21)

### Day 15-17
- Use system as primary record.
- Supervisor checks correction quality daily.

### Day 18-19
- Review low-stock alerts and purchase response speed.
- Improve response timeline.

### Day 20-21
- Conduct mini-audit on 10 key items.
- Document gaps and actions.

## Week 4: Optimization (Day 22-30)

### Day 22-24
- Track KPIs:
  - Stock accuracy %
  - Correction count
  - Stock-out events

### Day 25-27
- Fine-tune workflow (screen shortcuts, naming clarity).
- Identify next location for rollout.

### Day 28-29
- Final training refresh for all shifts.
- Create handover note.

### Day 30
- Management review and sign-off.
- Approve phase-2 rollout to next site.

## Daily 10-Minute Standup Format
- Yesterday issues
- Today risks
- Items below minimum stock
- Support needs

## Success Criteria by Day 30
- At least 95% same-day entry compliance.
- At least 98% stock accuracy on critical items.
- Clear correction approval trail.
- No major stock-out due to data mismatch.
