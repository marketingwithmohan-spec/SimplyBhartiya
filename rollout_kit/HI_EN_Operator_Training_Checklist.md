# Operator Training Checklist (Hindi + English)

## A) Basic / Buniyadi
- Can login/logout correctly.
- Sahi tarah login/logout kar pata hai.
- Understands item and unit.
- Item aur unit samajhta hai.

## B) Inward Entry
- Can create inward transaction.
- Inward transaction bana sakta hai.
- Can enter supplier/challan correctly.
- Supplier/challan sahi bhar sakta hai.

## C) Outward Entry
- Can create outward entry with purpose.
- Purpose ke saath outward entry kar sakta hai.

## D) Special Entries
- Can enter return/wastage.
- Return/wastage entry kar sakta hai.
- Knows when supervisor approval is needed.
- Jaanta hai kab supervisor approval chahiye.

## E) Reports
- Can open stock and daily summary reports.
- Stock aur daily summary reports dekh sakta hai.

## F) Readiness
- Same-day entry discipline followed.
- Same-day entry discipline follow karta hai.
