# 30-Day Go-Live Plan (Hindi + English)

## Week 1: Preparation / Taiyari
1. Pilot location finalize.
2. Roles assign: Operator, Supervisor, Manager.
3. Item master clean and opening stock upload.
4. Training sessions complete.

## Week 2: Controlled Go-Live / Niyantrit Shuruaat
1. Live entry start with paper parallel.
2. Daily closing stock comparison.
3. Frequent errors fix and retrain.

## Week 3: Stabilization / Sthirata
1. System as primary record.
2. Supervisor daily approval quality check.
3. Mini-audit on critical items.

## Week 4: Optimization / Sudhar
1. KPI review: accuracy, corrections, stock-outs.
2. Process fine-tuning.
3. Management sign-off and next-site planning.

## Day 30 Success / Safalta Maanak
- 95%+ same-day entry
- 98%+ critical stock accuracy
- Clear correction trail
